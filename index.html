Signs You Might Need a Break

Select a category to learn more.

How Your Body Feels

  • Tense muscles (shoulders, jaw)
  • Faster heartbeat
  • Short of breath
  • Feeling shaky or restless
  • Headache or feeling sick
  • Suddenly feeling very tired

How You Are Feeling

  • Feeling suddenly sad or angry
  • Feeling anxious
  • Feeling overwhelmed ("too much")
  • Feeling "numb" or disconnected

How You Are Thinking

  • Hard to focus
  • Having to re-read the same sentence
  • Feeling "spaced out" or "foggy"
  • Thinking about your own experiences
